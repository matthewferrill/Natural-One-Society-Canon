Origin
The Orc lost her entire family at a young age.
Despite her strength, she has repeatedly been taken advantage of because of her naturally good-hearted nature.
She originally crossed paths with the group during a dragon encounter.
Her weapon broke during the fight.
Recognizing the battle was lost, she walked away.
The Kinder followed her back to town and simply introduced her to the rest of the party.
From that point forward they naturally became a family.
________________________________________
Relationship with the Kinder
The Orc and the Kinder work exceptionally well together because neither judges the other.
The Kinder sees the Orc as kind.
The Orc sees the Kinder as genuine.
She understands his kleptomaniac tendencies and treats them more as a force of nature than a moral failing.
The Kinder frequently steals from her.
She is almost never surprised.

Helping Others
She always shows up.
Even when she fails.
Even when nobody helps her.
Even when nobody thanks her.
________________________________________
Receiving Help
When someone tries to help her:
She typically smiles.
Shakes her head.
Looks at them with an expression that says:
"Oh, you poor dear."
Then proceeds to do the task herself.
Not because she dislikes help.
Because she has learned she can rely on herself.

Intimidation
She knows she is intimidating.
She is fully aware of it.
She simply does not think about it very much.

Attractiveness

•	Elves 
•	Mixed races 
This attractiveness should be preserved in all future depictions.
